Hedgehog family



Eulipotyphlans are insectivorous mammals. Shrews and solenodons resemble mice, hedgehogs carry spines, gymnures look more like large rats, while moles are stout-bodied burrowers.

Mammal Life expectancy Size Weight
Hedgehog 5-6 Years 15-30cm 120g
Pygmy shrew 1 year ~10cm 4.1g
Mole 3-6 years 11-16cm 40-140g

Eulipotyphla features

  • Fairly small
  • Long, pointed snouts
  • Lots of sharp teeth
  • Small eyes
  • Pretty blind
