Carnivorans

There are over 260 species of carnivorans, the majority of which feed primarily on meat. They have a characteristic skull shape and dentition.

Carnivore features

  • Skill to hunt and kill prey
  • They're usually fast as they need to chase and catch prey
  • Sharp teeth
  • Sharp claws
  • Like meat(sorry vegans)
